What's up with Up?

The meaning of lines A horizontal line is sensed as “being at rest, just as the human body is at rest when horizontal. They give a sense of stability, balance, and tranquility.”1 A vertical line is sensed as “one of aspiration, reaching, assertiveness”2. Instead of the restful equilibrium you see with horizontal lines, we get a dynamic equilibrium. There are forces held in check, just like a human body standing takes lots of muscles to keep it upright, or the strength of a tree’s roots holding the tree against the wind.
